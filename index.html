Tech is for everyone

This website is meant to match students and underrepresented people in tech with tech conference tickets.
A huge part of the the industry is networking, and attending conferences.
It's how relationships are made. It's a way to display your interests. It's a way to learn.
If you are a student or an underrepresented person who is trying to break in to the tech industry, contact people in the Pledging section.

Making a difference

The problem with conferences. The people who attend already work in tech and have companies pay for their tickets.
Conference tickets can be expensive and it's a barrier of entry. Not everyone can afford a day off of work and an expensive conference ticket.
Let's make conferences accessible to everyone.

Long term goal

Conferences should have dedicated ticket blocks for students and people who are underrepresented in tech that they give away.
